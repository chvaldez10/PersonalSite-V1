Hello . . .

I received my B. Sc. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Calgary (UofC). When my friends ask my what I did in my degree, I say one word . . . circuits

I had a change of heart when it came to software after my internship at Computer Modelling Group Ltd. (CMG). Now, I am completing my Masters in Engineering (MEng) and hope to graduate in the spring of 2024.

Capstone Design Project

For my capstone project, I collaborated with 5 engineering students to design and validate the product Gensense which is capable of monitoring factors vital to indoor plant growth. The project was sponsored by Genoptic LED Inc. and supervised by professors from UofC.

MEng - Software

Last spring, I worked on a Spotify data analysis project with one of my friends. The project showcased a terminal based program that analyzed aggregate stats from an open source dataset. For the summer, I joined a group of students in my cohort with the goal of building community rating "web app".

QA Intern

For my internship, I worked as a software QA at CMG where I developed and managed multiple test suites. My favorite part of the internship was participating in a DeepRacer event hosted by CMG. The goal of the event was to create a reward function to train RC cars so that it can finish a lap as fast as possible.

Centre Assistant

Research Assistant
